Gay Lisp is the name given to the stereotype related with what people commonly call "gay voice". What is a gay voice? Do all gay people actually have a "gay voice"?

The documentary film "Do I sound gay?" explores this topic as you follow the director himself (David Thorpe) trying to find out more about it. This was the start point for this project.

You can check the trailer below.

There are many theories and studies about this topic, but none of them is conclusive. Some state that it has to do with mirroring, others say it's a myth and some others say that it's related to "show off" and attention seeking. This website will analyse some of the different theories.
